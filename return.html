Return Policy

Thank you for choosing our cafe as your dining destination. We take pride in providing you with fresh and delicious perishable goods. Please take a moment to familiarize yourself with our return policy below.

Eligibility for Returns:

Due to the nature of our perishable goods, we cannot accept returns or exchanges on food items unless they are spoiled, damaged, or incorrect.

Spoiled, Damaged, or Incorrect Items:

a. If you receive a spoiled, damaged, or incorrect food item, please notify our staff immediately or within 24 hours of purchase.
b. Provide details about the issue, along with any relevant photos or evidence.
c. We will investigate the matter and, if confirmed, offer a replacement or refund for the affected item.

Return Process:

a. For spoiled, damaged, or incorrect food items, please bring them back to our cafe along with the original receipt within 24 hours of purchase.
b. Our staff will assess the item and verify the issue.
c.If eligible, we will provide a replacement or issue a refund for the affected item.

Refunds:

a.Refunds for returned items will be issued in the original form of payment.
b. Please note that it may take several business days for the refund to appear on your bank statement, depending on your financial institution.

Non-Returnable Items:

Due to health and safety regulations, we cannot accept returns or exchanges for perishable food items that are not spoiled, damaged, or incorrect.

Satisfaction Guarantee:

We strive to provide you with the best dining experience possible. If you are unsatisfied with your meal, please notify our staff immediately, and we will do our best to address your concerns and ensure your satisfaction.

Please note that this return policy specifically applies to perishable food items purchased directly from our cafe. For returns of merchandise or non-perishable items, please refer to our general return policy or contact our staff for further assistance. If you have any questions or require further clarification regarding our return policy, please do not hesitate to contact our staff. We are here to assist you and ensure your dining experience is enjoyable. Thank you for choosing our cafe!
